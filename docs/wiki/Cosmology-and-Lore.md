# Cosmology & Lore

[← Home](Home.md) · full design doc: [`docs/cosmology.md`](../cosmology.md)

The mod is themed on **Walter Russell's cosmology** — the *two-way universe* of **rhythmic
balanced interchange**. It's **flavour, not physics**, but every block maps to one of
Russell's ideas, so the tech tree reads as a working model of his system.

## The premise

> *"The universe is a play of Light. All motion springs from a still magnetic centre of
> zero and returns to it, in equal giving and regiving."*

- **Light** is the one substance and the mod's energy. (Tracked internally as `ResonanceNode`
  / RU for save-compatibility; everywhere a player looks, it's **Light**.)
- **Stillness** is the source. Energy is not created or destroyed — it is *wound up* out of
  the still centre and *unwound* back into it.
- **Rhythmic balanced interchange** — everything is paired giving (**generation**,
  centripetal compression, *charging*) and regiving (**radiation**, centrifugal expansion,
  *discharging*). The grid is one long balanced interchange.
- **Octaves** — tones repeat in octaves. The 16 wireless channels *are* octaves; tuning a
  device sets its octave. Materials climb the octaves too (the Mote ladder, Radiant tier).

## How the gear maps

| In-world name | Concept | What it does |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Stillness Core** | the still centre of zero | slow baseline Light — "motion springs from rest" |
| **Resonant Coil** | generation / centripetal charging | winds ambient tone into stored Light |
| **Resonance Cell** | locked potential | banks large Light |
| **Wave / Dense / Octave Conduit** | the wave carries Light | wired transport — the wave is the unit of the cosmos |
| **Compressor** | compression = generative motion | presses matter into denser form (ore-doubling) |
| **Transmuter** | raising matter an octave | Light-powered smelting |
| **Growth / Warmth Radiator** | radiation / centrifugal outpouring | pours Light back as life and heat |
| **Polarity Field** | the two poles of one device | Attract (centripetal) / Repel (centrifugal) |
| **Balancer** | rhythmic balanced interchange | evens Light across all banks |
| **Wave Relay** + family | tones broadcast on an octave | wireless transport keyed by octave |
| **Storm Caller** | high-potential discharge wound back to Light | banks lightning during storms |
| **Octave Seed** | each octave's inert rest point | the catalyst that opens transmutation |
| **Radiant Ingot** | matter wound a full octave higher | the high-octave tier material |
| **Transmutation Table** | matter is condensed Light | dissolve ⇄ condense the Bound-Light economy |
| **Octave Star** | Light bound and carried | portable Bound-Light battery |
| **Resonant Thrusters** | centrifugal radiation = expansion | flight — the body thrown outward from centre |

## The narrative arc

The **[Great Work](The-Great-Work.md)** advancement tree retells this arc as you play:
mine matter, wind it into Light (generation), carry and bank it, pour it back out as work
(radiation), and finally transmute matter itself — generation and radiation held in
balance. Lore tooltips on the gear echo the same lines (*"Tuned to the octave — rhythmic
balanced interchange"*; *"The still magnetic centre of zero — all motion springs from
rest"*).
