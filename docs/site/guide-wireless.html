Wireless Transport

Move items, fluids, Light, and redstone over 16 channels (one per dye colour) — no conduits. Tune any device: right-click cycles mode (Receive → Send → Disabled), sneak-click steps the channel, or use a dye to jump to a colour.

Budget

Per channel, per tick: 8 items · 1 bucket · 1,000 Light per sender, capped at 64 / 8 / 16,000, all scaled by Wave Amplifier amplifiers (×2 each, ×16 cap). A channel needs ≥2 devices.
